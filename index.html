Understanding What Is In Our Orbit, and Where

While there are dozens of natural satellites in the solar system, for example our Moon, artificial satellites did not become a reality until the mid-20th century. The first artificial satellite was Sputnik, a small Russian probe that lifted off on Oct. 4, 1957. Since, we have added 8,260 individual satellites that orbit the Earth as of January 2022.
